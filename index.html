DocInsight

DocInsight was a passion project between me and my friends they we created during SBUHacks. The idea behind the web app, which was made using Bootstrap with a Node.js and Solidity backend, is that it will help patients share their information with doctors with the security of a blockchain system.

This web app would take a patient's information and store it in such a fashion such that any personal patient (i.e. name, birthday, etc.) information that is not strictly medically relevant can only be seen be a patient-approved doctor otherwise other doctors are allowed to see the anonymise mdeical information. This can be helpful for research purposes, enabling doctors to see a wide variety of information in order to seek out patterns behind medical problems without breaching privacy boundaries.
